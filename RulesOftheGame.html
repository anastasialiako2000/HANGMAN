HANGMAN

Rules of the Game

1.The player chooses if she/he wants to play, to see her/his results or exit.

2.If the player continues with play, the player has to guess a letter of the hidden word.

3.The player has 8 chances to find the word.

4.If the player enters a letter which doesn't have the correct format, the player doesn't lose one of the 8's chances.

5.The game finishes either when the player lost her/his 8 chances either when she/he found the hidden word. Then she/he can choose again to play, to see her/his results, or exit.
